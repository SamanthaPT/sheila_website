Cast
Sophie Oliver
Eve (Main Character)
Sophie’s first big debut was at the tender age of seven where she played ‘The Lion’ in Androcles and the Lion. This vital role required lots of psychological preparation especially as her face was concealed by a mascot head for the duration of the performance. Fifteen years later, Sophie graduated from The Arts University Bournemouth with a First Class BA Hons Degree in Acting. After graduating, Sophie toured the UK for seven months performing to over 25,000 young people about the dangers of alcohol abuse. Since moving to London in January, Sophie has continued to pursue her acting career, appearing in short films and fringe performances. She is particularly fond of comedies and has enjoyed playing parts such as Belinda in Seasons Greetings and Adriana in The Comedy of Errors. She makes regular trips to the theatre and can often be found at anything Shakespearian trying to sneak on stage in full costume.

Leonardo Celestino
Daniele (Italian Man)
Leonardo Celestino, was born and raised in Southern Italy and at the age of 19 he moved to Rome to pursue his acting career. After just a year of living in Rome he decided to move to London where he could be trained as an actor in English language and to have more opportunity in his field of work. After a year of doing part time courses he got a place at Drama school and trained with Christopher Fettes, Liana Nyquist and Giles Foreman. Following Drama school he has then signed with an Agent based in London.
Tom Coulston
Samuel (Friend 2)
Tom was born in the steel city of Sheffield in '84 with English, Scottish and Irish roots. As a teenager he was very interested in sports and played football to a good level, once playing at Bramall lane, the home of his team Sheffield United for the junior blades. After finishing school he studied sports science in Sheffield for 3 years and then started his career working in a gym becoming a personal trainer and boxercise instructor. Tom also started modeling at the age of 20 working for some well-known brands on the runway and print work. In May 2008 just before his 24th Birthday he transferred with his work to London to start a new chapter in his life! Tom decide to make a transition into acting when he was contacted by a director who wanted him to play a cameo role in his short film Are you proud of me? He is now training with his LA coach Michael John in London on a weekly basis and has found a real passion for acting.
Laura de Sommar
Anna (Friend 1)
Laura de Sommar is an actress living in London. In the past six months, she has had the pleasure to act in three short films including ‘Travel Sickness’ directed by Daphne Gastaldo, alongside an incredibly fun and talented crew. She is currently working on the feature film ‘In A Moment’ with director Sheila Silveira, as well as on an adaptation of ‘Love for Love’ by William Congreve with director James Kerr. Laura’s previous work includes five short films in Lima, Peru (Jun-Aug 2014). She also acted as ‘Fefu’ in the play called ‘Fefu and Her Friends’ written by Maria Irene Fornés. Directed by Marcia Ferguson, it was performed at the Annenberg Centre for Performing Arts in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania (Nov 2014). Before Laura became an actress, she studied International Business, at the University of Pennsylvania, a dual degree blending Wharton Business School and the College of Arts and Sciences. She realized that her true passion, acting, would only grow with the years. She triumphantly moved to London in January 2015 and has worked prolifically ever since.
Severine Vasselin
Chloe (Eve's boss)
Séverine is an international actress from Normandy, France. She is a seasoned traveller, has lived in several countries & has a Masters from York University in Toronto which she gained before undertaking film studies at the École Nationale Supérieure Louis Lumière. She has since turned to acting, using her language skills (she's fluent in French, English & German) to land international parts. She has been trained in the Meisner Method with Scott Williams at the Impulse Company & has studied with various international coachs such as Niki Flacks, Bob Mc Andrew & Giles Foreman. She is represented by agent Veronica Jacob at Fletcher Jacob in London.
Crew
Sheila Silveira
Writer/Director/Producer
Born in Canada to an Azorean imigrant family, however at the age of 3 they decided to move back to the Azores, Pico Island. Her love for film started at a very early age, when she was exposed to the Disney Films her family in Canada would send overseas. Living on a small island and not being exposed to many materialistic things, she held on to those films and imagined herself being a part of those worlds one day, by creating her own worlds. It was when she started imagining the stories she wrote, on screen, that she noticed there was nothing else that would give her as much joy than becoming a filmmaker. She decided to take a degree in Documentary Cinema which ended in 2013 at Escola Superior de Tecnologia de Abrantes, in Portugal. However she discovered that she wanted to further explore the production side of filmmaking, and is currently studying a MA in Film and TV Production at Bournemouth University where she is working on her first feature film as her masters project.
Marlene Fernandes
Director of Photography
Marlene Fernandes is 22 years old and comes from Portugal. She knew she wanted to study cinema when she saw Alfred Hitchcock´s movies. She ended up doing a degree in Documentary Cinema and was also part of the Organization of an Experimental Film Festival- INDEX in Portugal. After her degree she decided to study something more specific in the area so she moved to London, and since she's been interested in street photography for a long time, she chose to do a MA in Cinematography at Bournemouth University to develop her skills on moving pictures.
Asher Abraham
Sound Designer
Asher Abraham is a sound designer, location sound recordist and boom operator. Hailing from the southern state of India, Kerala, he specialises in designing, recording and mixing sound for motion pictures. He has a passion to excel. Asher’s core competencies are excellence, creativity and integrity. He has a BSc (Hons.) in Music and Audio Technology from Bournemouth University, where he’s currently pursuing a MA in Sound Production for Film and Television. He loves sports, photography and watching critically acclaimed movies and television shows. Apart from designing and recording sound, Asher also plays the guitar, design websites and graphics in his spare time.
Giovanni Todeschini
Editor
Giovanni born in Brescia, Italy in 1989. When he was exposed at the tender age of four to Jurassic Park he knew he wanted to become a palaeontologist and bring the dinosaurs back to life. A few years later, upon watching the "making of" video of the same film, he decided that working with cameras and special effects would have been a lot more fun. In 2012 he graduated with a BA in Film and Broadcast Production from London Metropolitan University, where he learned how much of the film grammar can be observed and changed in the editing room. He is currently pursuing a MA in Post-Production Editing at Bournemouth University.
